ABOUT
The Mission

BCTF is designed to be an institution that functions, preserves capital, and accumulates Bitcoin twenty years from now.

Problem

Why traditional DAOs fail

Whale capture, governance apathy, vote buying, short-term profit seeking, treasury extraction, and social manipulation are the common failure modes BCTF is explicitly designed to resist.

Solution

Constitutional + Autonomous

An Autonomous Governor operates inside a written constitution. Community input is a signal, not a command. The treasury cannot be captured because the rules are enforced by code, not trust.

Governance

Earned influence

Economic ownership and governance influence are deliberately separated. Reputation is earned through sustained contribution — research, security reviews, and proposal quality — not simply by holding the most capital.

Guiding Principle

No hype optimization

BCTF is not optimized for token price, TVL rankings, or launch excitement. It is optimized for being a trustworthy institution in 2045 — when the incentives to attack it will be far greater than they are today.
